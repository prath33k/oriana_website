Artiste Cove

“Art is the only way to run away without leaving home”

The Picasso

“Every artist dips his brush into his own soul and paints his own nature in his pictures”

This is basically you on a canvas. No restrictions, no limitations.

  • 1. A theme will be provided a week before the fest and the completed art work is to be submitted on the first or second days of the fest.

  • 2. Participants also have the option of creating or completing their artwork after reaching the venue. In such cases a 3 hours will be allowed to the participant.

  • 3. Participants should bring their own materials required for the artwork

  • 4. Participants should take care not to write their name or signature on the artwork

  • 5. From water colours to oil paint to acrylic to paper or cloth or canvas, to sketching to doodling, it is a vast ocean in front of you to let the artist within you to come out and go crazy!

  • Registration fee: Rs.100

Best Out of Waste

Creativity is an intelligent mind having fun. Let’s put that brain of yours to good use as you ponder over how to create the best of art from the worst of materials. Go on and start collecting all those waste you threw away thinking you’re done with it, you’re not quite done with it yet!

  • 1. The competition would consist of a team of 2 members.

  • 2. The team would have to create an art form (3D) out of day-to-day waste materials collected (tissues, plastic cans, cork screws etc.).

  • 3. The team may bring with them any waste material they think is suited for their art work (as long as it is waste).

  • 4. Use of any electronic gadgets or other references is strictly prohibited.

  • 5. No theme as such is present for the competition (let your imagination get the better of you!)

  • 6. Time limit: 180 minutes
