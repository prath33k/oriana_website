Artiste Cove

“Art is the only way to run away without leaving home”

The Picasso

“Every artist dips his brush into his own soul and paints his own nature in his pictures”

This is basically you on a canvas. No restrictions, no limitations.

Mehendi

No Indian would be alien to the beauty of a hand entwined with elegant mehendi designs. The symbolic representation of inner sun, Mehendi brings to our mind times of celebration, joy and harmony.

Face Painting

How well can you paint without a canvas? Time to find out with the ever fun competition of face painting where you master your skills on the face of a friend (poor chap!)

Quilling

This art requiring skilful handwork, extreme patience and limitless creativity, here you shape your imagination into life with colourful strips of paper.

Best Out of Waste

Creativity is an intelligent mind having fun. Let’s put that brain of your to good use as you ponder over how to create the best of art from the worst of materials. Go on and start collecting all those waste you threw away thinking you’re done with it, you’re not quite done with it yet!

Poster Making

Express your thoughts and ideas in the form of colours, words and wit.

Rangoli

Any auspicious occasion is incomplete without a beautiful design of colours carpeting the floor. The bright colours, vivid designs and intricate patterns are the standout of any beautiful rangoli design.

Water Colouring

Splash of colour and unlimited imagination.

Sketching

Bring out your creativity in black and white.
