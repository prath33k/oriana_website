VERVE

Here’s to all dancers whose real home is the stage, for we bring to you VERVE, an all genre dance competition, the flagship event of ORIANA ‘17. So, if you think you can dance, we have an opportunity of a lifetime for you to flaunt your best moves, groove on your favourite tunes and shake yourselves loose!

Dance Til' Ya Drop

So, you believe your team has got what it takes? Well then, pump up and get your game face on for we have got exactly the kind of platform you are looking for with talents all over the nation. Bring out your inner creativity and dance like there is no tomorrow!

Depict a theme with your moves, and rock the stage with your steps adopting a eastern, eastern fusion, salsa, hip-hop, pop, contemporary, b-boying, or cinematic style.

  • Category: Group

  • Qualifiers

  • In qualifiers, you are required to submit a video clipping of your performance of max 4 minutes.

  • Participating team should upload the video to google drive and create a shareable link and forward the link to verve@orianaaiims.com or Whatsapp it to +919072827550, mentioning the name of the event, name of your group, and the name of your college. The video is to be submitted on or before 15th October. Finalists will be screened on the video sent and the selected teams will be notified by 17th October.

  • Finals

  • Top 10 teams selected from the qualifiers shall compete in the finals by performing live on stage at ORIANA ’17.

  • Registration Fee: Rs.100 per team member

  • 1. Participants must be college students. They should carry their valid college ID card with them.

  • 2. Participation has to be in a group. Group has to be of minimum 4 and maximum 12 people.

  • 3. Both theme or non-theme performances are allowed.

  • 4. Time limit: Maximum of 12 minutes (includes stage in to stage out). Music will be stopped exactly after 10 minutes of performance.

  • 5. Props (if used), has to be brought by the respective teams. Use of breakable items, fire, water, sharp objects or heavy items are strictly not allowed. No kind of stage settings are allowed.

  • 6. Pre-recorded sound track has be brought in MP3 format only and has to be submitted before the commencement of the event. Please carry the soundtrack on at least 2 pen drives.

  • 7. The teams are required to report at the venue at least 1 hour before the event starts.

  • 8. The participating teams are requested to ensure the dignity of their performances. Indecency or vulgarity of any kind will lead to disqualification of the team.

  • 9. Decision of the judges and coordinators shall be final and binding on all the teams. There shall be no arguments in this regard.

  • 10. Oriana ’17 organizing committee holds the right to modify the rules at any moment of time.

Nrutya

Delve into the depths of our grand heritage through the diverse traditional dance forms as you witness/be a part of our NRUTYA, ORIANA ’17. Enthral the audience as you merge with the rhythm, or let yourself be raptured by the mudras as elegance blends with legacy and escape to eternal bliss. Cherish the stage with the best of classical, semi-classical or folk to make the crowd groove to your beats.

  • Category: Group or Solo(Each will have separate judgements)

  • Qualifiers

  • In qualifiers, you are required to submit a video clipping of your performance of max 3 minutes.

  • The dance video should not be an “on stage performance” and the dance form should be classical, s emi-classical or folk.

  • Participating individual/team should upload the video to google drive and create a shareable link and forward the link to verve@orianaaiims.com or Whatsapp it to +919072827550, mentioning the name of the event, name of the contestant/group, and the name of your college. The video is to be submitted on or before 15th October.Finalists will be screened on the video sent and the selected contestants will be notified by 17th October.

  • Finals

  • The top 15 selected from the qualifiers shall compete in the finals. Out of them winners will be declared from each category.

  • Registration Fee

  • Solo: Rs.200

  • Group: Rs.100 per team member

  • 1. Participants must be college students. They should carry their valid college ID card with them.

  • 4. Time limit: Maximum of 6 minutes (includes stage in to stage out). Music will be stopped exactly after 5 minutes of performance.

  • 5. Props (if used), has to be brought by the respective contestant/group. Use of breakable items, fire, water, sharp objects or heavy items are strictly not allowed. No kind of stage settings are allowed.
