Register

Futsal

Think quick, move quick and play quick as it's futsal.

Register

  • 1. Online registration must be completed for participating.

  • 2. Players without their respective college id cards will not be allowed to play under any circumstances.

  • 3. Matches will be separately conducted for girls and boys.

  • 4. Teams are supposed to have their own team jersey.

  • 5. A college is allowed to have maximum 2 boys’ team and a girls’ team.

  • 6. It is mandatory for boys and girls to wear boots and shoes respectively while playing.

  • 7. Total duration of the match is 30 min(15+15).

  • 8. A team consists of total 7 players, five in the field (of which one will be goalkeeper) and two rotational substitutes.

  • 9. A goalkeeper is not allowed to use hands for stopping the ball. In such cases penalty will be awarded to the opposite team.

  • 10. In case of draw after 30 minutes, no extra time will be given. The game will lead to penalty shootout.

  • 11. Goalkeeping is not allowed during penalty shootout.

  • 12. In case of a draw even after shootout, one penalty is awarded to each team and the team which scores first will be the winners.

  • 13. Decision taken by the referee is final and binding.

  • Registration fee: Rs.500 per team.

Cricket

A gentleman's game on its prowl.

Register

  • 1. Online registration must be completed for participating.

  • 2. Players without their respective college ID cards will not be allowed to play under any circumstances.

  • 4. First 3 overs will be powerplay (3 players allowed outside the inner ring).

  • 5. There will be a batting powerplay of 1 over( 4 players outside the ring).

  • 6. During the rest of the overs 5 players are allowed outside the ring.

  • 7. The game will be played with the red tennis ball (Nivia).

  • 8. The participating teams are required to bring their own Cricket kits.

  • 9. Pitch is ace to the standard measures.

  • 10. Extras such as no ball and wide are included, while overthrow, bye and leg bye are not included.

  • 11. 1 bowler can bowl 3 overs, and the rest can bowl 2 overs.

  • 12. Umpires decision is final and binding.

Basketball

It's not how big you are, it's how big you play.

Register

  • 1. Online registration must be completed for participating.

  • 2. Players without their respective college id cards will not be allowed to play under any circumstances.

  • 3. FIBA rules are applicable.

  • 4. Maximum of 12(5+7) players are allowed in a team.

  • 5. Each team member should be in proper numbered (4-15) team kit.

  • 6. A team member is entitled to play when his name has been entered on the score-sheet before the start of the game and as long as he has neither been disqualified nor committed five (5) fouls.

  • 7. Any equipment that is designed to increase a player’s height or reach or in any other way give an unfair advantage is not permitted. Also equipment’s that can harm other players are not allowed.

  • 8. Matches will have 4 quarters with 10 minutes for each quarter(unless otherwise stated).

  • 9. If a team is unable to field five players till 15 minutes after the scheduled starting time of the game or if the team refuses to play the match then the match is awarded to the opponent team.

  • 10. Each team shall report at the match venue 30 minutes prior to the scheduled start of their match.

  • 11. Decision of the referee shall be final and binding.
