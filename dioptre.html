Dioptre

Dioptre is a photography contest that sources and showcases outstanding work from amateurs and established artists alike. Photography is a perfect instrument which exhibits author's deep sensitivity and need to express feelings through an image. It is not the technical prowess that solely determine a perfect shot but the author's calibre to find something intriguing out of an otherwise ordinary moment. It has little to do with the thing you see and everything to do with how you see them.
