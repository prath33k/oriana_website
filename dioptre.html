Dioptre

Dioptre is a photography contest that sources and showcases outstanding work from amateurs and established artists alike. Photography is a perfect instrument which exhibits author's deep sensitivity and need to express feelings through an image. It is not the technical prowess that solely determine a perfect shot but the author's calibre to find something intriguing out of an otherwise ordinary moment. It has little to do with the thing you see and everything to do with how you see them.

  • Event Guidelines

  • Themes for the event :

  • Emotions

  • Reflection

  • Water Drops

  • It will be a single round online event.

  • Participants are allowed a maximum of 3 entries (1 entry per theme).

  • Entrants should mail their photographs pertaining to aforementioned themes to dioptre@orianaaiims.com before 20:00 on 22nd October 2017.

  • Rules

  • 1. The event is open to all college students with the limit of one entry per theme.

  • 2. The picture should be saved as “Participant’s college_Participant’s name.jpg”

  • 3. File should be in jpeg/jpg format.

  • 4. The entries should be accompanied by a caption not more than 20 words.

  • 5. Copyright: The copyright of all images submitted to the competition will remain with the owner who will be credited. Each entrant grants the organisers the right to use their images in relation to the competition, if required.

  • 6. There must not be any watermarks, copyright marks or any text/graphic in the picture.

  • 7. Please limit your adjustments to minor changes of cropping, sharpening and contrast. Avoid significant over or under colour saturation. Dodging (to brighten shadows) or burning (to darken highlights) are allowed, but the alterations should enable the photograph to closely resemble what you see. Please avoid any extensive manipulation.

  • 8. The participants should retain the original image as it may be asked upon by the organisers.

  • 9. The minimum resolution of pictures should be 2000pixels on the shorter side. If the image size exceeds the normal e-mail limit of 25MB, upload it on google drive and create a shareable link, then email the link.

  • 10. Stitched panoramas are allowed.

  • 11. The photo should not have been previously entered in any competition or exhibition.

  • 12. Plagiarism is strictly prohibited and any entrant found indulging in such activity shall be disqualified immediately.

  • 13. Evaluation criteria is as follows:

  • Judge's Ratings - 50%

  • Caption - 10%

  • Facebook Likes - 40%

  • 14. Each theme will have its own individual winner with an overall three winners.

  • 15. The decision of the organisers shall be final and binding to all contestants.

  • 16. The photographs submitted by all participants shall be uploaded in our facebook page

  • 17. Photographs will be updated in the page everyday once in between 7PM to 11PM. Once uploaded the participant will be acknowledged through an e-mail.
