Crescendo

Are you ready to make the crowd go wild ? Oriana'17 brings you Crescendo, a battle of the bands. So, tune your strings, clear your throats and head over to Oriana '17 and show the crowd what you've got.

Register

  • 1. Crescendo welcomes bands from all genres of music. College ID is mandatory for registration.

  • 2. Each band should have a minimum of 3 members. No more than 8 members will be allowed in a band.

  • 3. A 5 piece drum set, amplifiers, microphones, microphone stand will be made available to the participating bands. Please do bring your own cymbals, single and double bass pedals, and stool for your performance. These will not be provided.

  • 4. Each band will be allotted a period of 5 + 15 minutes in total.

  • 5. Pre recorded beats/ rhythm/ songs on synthesizer or any other device will not be allowed. Use of such, if found will amount to disqualification of the band from the competition.

  • 6. Oriana will have exclusive right to use any of the music tracks for future promotional purposes, all though due credits to the band will always be mentioned.

  • 7. Any discrepancies concerning the event will be resolved by Team Crescendo. The decisions made by Team Crescendo will be considered final.

  • 8. Oriana reserves all rights to change any specification of the event including rules and regulations, bands selected, prize money and timings if necessary. All decisions by Oriana shall be final and binding

  • Registraion Fee: Rs.1300
