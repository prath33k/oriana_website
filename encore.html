Malhar

“Where idols are born”

For those gifted with the extraordinary power to move hearts and touch souls with their voices, here is the chance to set yourself apart from the crowd.

ORIANA’17 presents MALHAR, where the VOICE emerges from the echoes.

Register

  • Both solo and duet performances are included in this event.

  • Auditions

  • 1. Preliminary auditions will be held one day before the main event.

  • 2. Online registration must be done for participating in the event. Last date for online registration is 26th October

  • 3. Participants can choose a song of their choice (English/Hindi- no other languages allowed) of 2 minutes for the audition.

  • 4. For duet competition, both the participants should sing.

  • 5. Karaoke and instruments are not allowed for the auditions.

  • 6. The participants selected will be sent a SMS on the same day.

  • 7. Judging criteria: vocal abilities of the singer.

  • Main Event

  • 1. Participants can choose a song of their own choice(English/Hindi only).

  • 2. Karaoke and instruments are allowed.

  • 3. In solo, in the event of using an instrument, the participant should play it himself/herself and not bring an accompanist.

  • 4. Time limit: 6 minutes (inclusive of sound check).

  • 5. Judging criteria: vocal abilities, song selection and stage presence.

  • 6. The decision of the judges and coordinators shall be final and binding in all cases.

  • Registration fee: Rs.100 each

Saarang

“Where words fail, music speaks”

Music is a language without words and those who speak to us through it are living embodiments of the magic this great power offers.

ORIANA ’17 presents SAARANG where you can flaunt your skills in acoustic music

Register

  • Auditions

  • 1. Audition is online.

  • 2. A pre-recorded video of the performance, of 2 minutes, should be attached while registering online.

  • 3. No vocals are allowed.

  • 4. Use of electrical instruments (except keyboard) will not be considered.

  • 5. The last date for registering online is 26th October.

  • 6. The participants selected for the main event will be sent a SMS/email informing them the same.

  • Main Event

  • 1. Participants should bring their own instruments to perform at the main event.

  • 2. No electrical instruments (except keyboard) are allowed.

  • 3. No vocals are allowed.

  • 4. Timelimit : 10minutes(inclusive of soundcheck)

  • 5. The decision of the judges and coordinators shall be final and binding in all cases.

  • Registration fee : Rs.100
