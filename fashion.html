A La Mode

Join us for evening of glamour , fashion and style and treat yourself to the fashion extravaganza of men and women from all over the country. See and be seen at the most stylish event of the year and leave with style goals for days as life's too short to wear boring clothes!

Register

  • 1. Each college/institute can have only a single team taking part in the event.

  • 2. Each team can have a maximum of 15 participants.

  • 3. Time limit for the event is 10 minutes (8 minute for the show and 2 minute for stage setting)

  • 4. The theme is open to the choice of the teams participating.

  • 5. Music for the ramp walk must be submitted in 2 separate pen drives at least 30 minutes before the show.

  • 6. Any objects causing destruction to the stage and stage settings is strictly prohibited.

  • 7. Any level of vulgarity or obscenity will not be tolerated during the show and the team would face immediate disqualification (i.e. the show will be interrupted and will be discontinued then and there)

  • 8. The decision of the judges and co-ordinators will be final and binding in all occasions.

  • Participation Fee: Rs.150 per person
