Campus Princess

"A journey of a thousand miles begins with a single step."

Campus Princess is a breeding ground for empowering young talent by enabling them to realizing their dream of becoming a beauty queen. Scheduled twice in a calender year, the winners of Campus Princess gets an auto-entry to the top 24 finalist of Miss India and Miss Diva pageants respectively. So do you have what it takes to go all the way and be the next Miss India?

Register

  • 1. Nationality : Indian passport holder

  • 2. Age : Between 18 - 25 years(as on 31st December 2017)

  • 3. Relationship status : Unmarried/Single/Not engaged

  • 4. Height : 5'5" and above(without heels)

  • 5. Student of any recognized educational instituition.

  • 6. If selected, the applicant will have to follow the dress code of a Black Cocktail Dress and Stilettos while walking the ramp.
